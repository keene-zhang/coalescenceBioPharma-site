Founded in June 2022, Coalescence BioPharma, LLC is a private biotech consulting company located in the City of Vestavia Hills, Alabama, USA. The company provides consulting services for Biotech/Pharmaceutical companies to reach their clinical operations and business development aims.

Business Scopes

  • Design, implementation, and execution of project plan based on client goals, analysis and interpretation of project data, and recommendation for future progression
  • Assistance on shaping overall team and driving efforts for multiple programs in a highly efficient, fast-paced environment
  • Providing essential business development support to pharmaceutical industrials for partnership and specific platform acquisition or license in/out in multi-national environments
  • Providing essential support for clinical trials in USA including IND application, clinic study design, and clinic trial site selection and coordination
